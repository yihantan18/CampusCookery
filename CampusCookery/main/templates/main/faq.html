Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can vegetarians ensure sufficient protein intake?
  • Legumes and soy products - e.g., soybeans, tofu, soy milk.
  • Nuts and seeds - e.g., almonds, cashews, pumpkin seeds.
  • Whole grains - e.g., oats, quinoa, brown rice.
  • Dairy and eggs - Greek yogurt, cheese, milk.
Q: Can burnt stir-fry be saved?
  • Lightly burnt: Transfer to a new pan immediately.
  • Mild burnt taste: Add vinegar or lemon juice.
  • Severe burnt taste: Best to discard.
Q: How to prevent pasta from sticking together?
  • Use **plenty of boiling water**.
  • Add **pasta after water boils**.
  • Stir frequently during the first **2 minutes**.
  • Mix with sauce immediately.
Q: What food combinations should be avoided?
  • **Spinach + Tofu**: Can block calcium absorption.
  • **Seafood + Vitamin C**: Risk of arsenic reactions.
  • **Milk + Lemon**: May cause indigestion.
  • **Tea/Coffee + Milk**: Tannins hinder digestion.
Q: What to do if the cake is too dry?
  • Brush with **syrup or honey**.
  • Add **cream or jam**.
  • Use **microwave steaming** with hot water.
Q: Why is my rice too wet or too hard?
  • Too wet: Let excess water evaporate, stir gently.
  • Too hard: Add a little hot water and steam.
